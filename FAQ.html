FAQ's on Aesthetic Treatments

Is it better to have a Hollywood smile?

Barad Dental vitalizes the natural look. However, if you have tried less aggressive solutions like bleaching and/or polishing with no desired results, you might want to consider the Hollywood Smile. If you feel uncertain or self-aware about your smile or if you believe a Hollywood Smile would boost your confidence, Barad Dental would be honoured to help.

Does Zoom bleaching hurt the teeth?

No. There were numerous studies to monitor any possible damage of the structure of the teeth after Zoom Bleaching. As the leading clinic in the dental industry, Barad Dental strives to attain at the supreme edge regarding studies in science & technology in every single field of dentistry. And no, not a single study showed a significant damage of the tooth structure after bleaching.
