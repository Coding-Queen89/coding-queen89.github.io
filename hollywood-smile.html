What are Hollywood Smiles?

Do you want to shine with a smile like Julia Roberts? Or jump off fighter jets with dazzling white teeth like Tom Cruise? Seeking a smile at Barad Dental Clinic is a decision you won't regret. Here, we intertwine multiple dental procedures to magically alter your smile to that of a star!
With top-tier dentists, you will wear your smile with unwavering confidence.

Attributes & Societal Trends

Not everyone with a bright smile has had a Hollywood make-over. Some features characterize a Hollywood Smile over all others and they include:

  • Super smooth surfaces
  • Ultra-white smiles and/or different shades of white teeth
  • Thick white layer, more opaque than translucent

We get it! You want a bright beautiful smile! But trust us not every white smile is a genuine one. If your dentists couldn't balance out the correct white proportions to make your smile look natural, everyone will know you visited a dentist! 😉 Don't worry we've got you covered.

Natural-looking teeth are mesmerizing due to their complexity and thick ultra-white smiles aren't that difficult. At Barad Dental though, our techinicians can vary the depth of the white layers to make them seem just as translucent as your enamel. Have you noticed how most smile makeovers are extremely smooth while your natural teeth have bumps? You can feel them if you run your finger along your front tooth. These bumps help reflect and refract light more efficiently than one smooth surface.
Our dentists and technicians work hard to re-create those bumps and allow you to have the best smile in KSA!

Smile Transformation

As previously mentioned, to initiate a Hollywood smile transformation, we merge two or more dental procedures for the final fanatical outcome. Albeit taking up a large part of the Hollywood smile, dental veneers aren't the only applicances that are utilized. Crowns enhance your teeth's aesthetic nature as well as protecting them from future decay and/or damage. Zoom bleaching alongside polishing removes plaque (leftover food particles that decolorize your teeth), leaving your teeth's enamel with a clean, pearl-white sheen.

Polishing & Bleaching

If you love drinking tea or coffee, you might have realised a slight discoloration of the outer part of your teeth(enamel). The yellow layer can also be due to neglected dental hygiene for elongated periods of time. As an elite patient, you should not reach this level, but if you do, Dr. Waad Matrafi, an extraordinary dentist, will gladly perform Zoom Bleaching to regain your teeth brightness with a special discount.

Additional teeth polishing may be required if we still notice its necessity to retain the natural color.

Dental Veneers

"Hollywood smiles are mostly either veneers or full crowns." Presenting the two most predominant dental procedures as plausible options for a smile makeover, Dr. Karam Al-Zagzoug, a surgical dentist and an Ibn Sina National College For Medical Studies graduate, expresses his disappointment when a dear patient visits with damaged teeth due to a failed makeover from a neighboring clinic. "We had to put in extra hours to reform the veneer molds and impressions with digital technology. While the final replacement was in progress, we applied a set of temporary veneers instead."

Esthetic Crowns

Crowns are protective coverings crafted by our technicians to restore a tooth's function and appearance. A smile makeover may need crown fabrication if the tooth is damaged or has previously recieved a root canal treatment.

Dr. Ibrahim Al-Shahri, for example, magnificently transformed one patient's decayed teeth to a smile that challenges the "Hollywood" stars. Unlike veneers that are a set of layers covering the teeth's outer part only, crowns and bridges cover the entire tooth's sides hence protecting it from further infection by bacteria or micro-organisms.

Clear Aligners - Invisalign

It is not impossible that your teeth are finely shaped and taken care of when a slight, perhaps genetic, factor causes teeth misalignment. Our orthodontists will cover you by avoiding the robust metallic braces. Clear aligners like Invisalign close gaps between your teeth using transparent removable sets.

Limitations

Despite the boost of confidence we get when our smiles are big and bright, Barad Dental doesn't always advise its patients with a Hollywood Smile Makeover because no matter how advanced our world becomes, God's nature will forever overshadow human capabilities.
Some customers who aren't suggested to go for a Hollywood Smile are:

  • Discouraging Teeth Status: If your teeth have cavies or are seriously damaged, our dentists may present the option of a root canal treatment first.
  • Age Restriction: Although Barad Dental strives to enhance your self-esteem, if your teeth aren't fully mature yet, we don't easily see to a Hollywood Smile as it's more probable than not that your teeth will develop into a dashing smile!
