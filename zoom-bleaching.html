Zoom Bleaching

Brushing your teeth 3 or more times a day will keep your smile brightly beautiful and make you feel more self-assured and confident. But let's face it: The hustle and bustle of life makes most of us neglect those 2 extra minutes! Accordingly, our teeth get a little stained with less care and more colored liquids like tea and coffee.
Don't worry! Barad Dental will ensure you regain your mesmerizing white smile within 45 minutes of Zoom Bleaching.

How it Works

Whitening your teeth in our clinic is the easiest way to reclaim your smile. Before bleaching, we will check your teeth for any material that might be affected with the process such as fillings or crowns.
"Once everything is ready, we apply a whitening gel to our patients' teeth which works to break down intrinsic and extrinsic stains," Dr. Waad Al-Matrafi, a general dentist at Barad Dental, clarifies. She further explains the whitening-gel contains Hydrogen Peroxide (H2O2) which acts as a bleaching agent activated by Zoom light.
"I usually take one session, two cycles. It could be more depending on the results that satisfy the patients," Dr. Waad adds. "But most of the time, people feel pleased after two cycles!"

Why Zoom Bleaching

Most of Barad Dental's patients are Elites and take utmost care of their oral health. However, their hectic schedules may divert them a little and Zoom Bleaching is perfect. If you are an Elite, claim your secret Zoom Bleaching Discount calling us here.

  • More Convenient: Within one visit to Barad, your teeth will shine once again.
  • Customizable to your needs: Our dentists will adjust the intensity of the whitening according to what YOU see fit.
  • Long Lasting Results: Zoom Bleaching lasts ups to 24 months and 36 months if you are one of our Elite Patients who brush their teeth 3 times a day.

Safety Considerations

Barad's dentists will take care of any cavities, fillings, and/or gum diseases before proceeding. Additionaly, if the patient is pregnant or nursing, we advice them to postpone any teeth whitening procedures until after childbirth or breastfeeding.
At Barad Dental, our patients don't experience sensitivity from Zoom Bleaching due to the intense tenderness of our dentists. As one of the best practitioners of Zoom Bleaching and an experienced dentist, Dr. Waad Al-Matrafi proudly declares, "Regarding desensitization, till now no patient experienced any sensitivity. However, if they did, it depends on what type of agents we have in our clinic. If it’s moderate to severe pain, then I’ll recommend paracetamol."
