What is Pit & Fissure Sealant?

Dental implants are synthetic titanium-made tooth roots inserted (implanted) in the jawbone (endosteal) or above the jawbone (subperiosteal) instead of a missing tooth root. Upon these implants, dental crowns are placed, hence called: implant-supported crowns. With the correct application, our dentists will ensure perfect teeth replacement, restoration and jawbone structure.

When do I Need the Sealant?

Perhaps due to a root canal treatment or tooth extraction, the root couldn't be salvaged and was removed. Trauma like car accidents may also damage the tooth, calling for either of the previous treatments. And while dental bridges replace missing teeth, you might need an implant if necessary.
