Pit & Fissure Sealant

Nobody does it better than us!

Your molars and premolars are designed to chew down on the food you eat. The grooves found in them might help you and might destroy you! Microorganisms can thrive in the presence of accumulated food in your teeth. Thus, to prevent tooth decay, Barad Dental applies a protective layer called a fissure sealant that prevents teeth and food leftovers from sticking to the grooves of your teeth!
