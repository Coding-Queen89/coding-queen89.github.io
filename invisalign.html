Clear Aligners - Invisalign

More than 5000 years ago, the Ancient Egyptians thought of metal wires to right misaligned teeth, and throughout history we have continued using metals. But no more! With the advancement of technology, clear plastic appliances can be molded to fit over your teeth and correct their alignment. At Barad Dental, we provide the widest and most infamous forms of clear aligners: Invisalign. Transparent, comfortable and removable, clear aligners and/or Invisalign overshadow traditional metal braces . Or do they? Perhaps, there is more to metallic braces than meets the eye!

Inside Our Clinic

Utilizing our most advanced technology in 3D printing ever to exist in KSA, we will scan your teeth and re-create your mouth into a precise digital model. Tailored only for you, the molded model is fit to your mouth and perfectly forces your teeth into a beautiful smile. Our Elite Patients visit us every 2 to 3 weeks for supreme results.

Braces VS Invisalign

Braces are stainless steel brackets "glued" to each tooth and braced firmly in place by metallic wires. Whilst aligners are a series of trays custom-made for our patients and swapped every 2 weeks for the next ones in the set. Which one is better? Can you always replace clear aligners with braces? Do you think your age plays a role in your choice?

Benefits

Like mouthguards, Invisalign aligners are silken on your gums and lips contradicting metal braces that cut your mouth and scathe your cheek's insides.

You can remove the clear aligners whenever you want, but keep in mind: 20 hours is the minimum time you should keep them on. This advantage is a setback in and of itself. It becomes an obligation for you to take them out before eating which is somewhat embarassing for some and before re-fitting them in place, you should brush your teeth after every meal.
With braces, on the other hand, you can enjoy most foods without worrying about removal.

Suitability

We, at Barad Dental, desire no dental fear to develop in our teenage patients, hence always keeping in mind the fear of rejection from visible metallic braces and social anxiety, we usually recommend Invisalign to our younger customers.

Our orthodontist will tell you that braces are more suitable for teeth rotated more than 20 degrees as Invisalign can fix tiny teeth rotations, but are rendered useless for severely rotated teeth.

Costs & Prices

Due to their more aesthetic nature and popularity, dentists charge more for Invisalign than they do metal braces. Barad Dental wants to see you first and discuss pricing at your terms! Maintenance and Care

Taking care of your aligners ensures they keep that lustrous tranparency to them. Everytime you remove your aligners, rinse them with lukewarm water to prevent buildup of bacteria. Stay clear from hot waters that can distort them. You can also clean them with soft brushes and Invisalign cleaning solution that you will recieve at Barad Dental. Beware of toothpastes, colored soaps or being too harsh on your Invisalign aligners so as not to make them more noticeable.
